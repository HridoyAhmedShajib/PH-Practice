My Travel Blog

Cox’s Bazar, the world’s longest sea beach, is a mesmerizing blend of golden sands, rolling waves, and breathtaking horizons. My journey began with stunning sunrises over the Bay of Bengal, followed by visits to Himchari Waterfall and the serene Inani Beach. Evenings brought the joy of savoring fresh seafood, from grilled prawns to spicy crab curry, at charming beach side stalls. As night fell, the beach transformed into a stargazer’s paradise, where the hum of waves and glittering skies created a sense of pure tranquility. Cox’s Bazar is more than a destination—it’s an unforgettable escape into nature’s embrace.
